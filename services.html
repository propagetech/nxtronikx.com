Services

Asset Procurement and Secure Logistics

We are capable of procuring technology assets such as desktop computers, laptops and mobile devices, Servers, Switches and other IT products. Our pan- India logistics network maximises asset security through the usage of nationwide service agreements with trusted long-time Logistics Service Providers who ensure complete protection of assets throughout the recovery process. Assets are individually tagged and tracked providing seamless physical and data security protection. We use closed containers with one-time lock and individual packaging for high value goods and offer real-time asset tracking through the transit as value-added services. Our material is insured and goes through a closed-loop chain of custody of assets.

Further, our secure facilities are enabled with stringent safety protocols including CCTV cameras, movement sensors and multiple 24/7 security personnel. Asset Testing, Refurbishing, Repairs and Enablement. Our asset refurbishing services for computers and mobile devices include functional testing and assessment of assets, refurbishment to ‘service grade’ standard, cosmetic refinishing of units, out-of-warranty repairs and servicing in a short turnaround time. Furthermore, we are also capable of recovery of high-value parts contained within mobiles, desktops and laptop computers, which can be renewed and fed into any of the other streams to add value and increase remarketing revenue.

Deployment and Decommissioning

NTPL provides a range of technical solutions including pre-staging, configuration, testing, imaging, pre-assembly, deployment and decommissioning. NTL provides fully qualified technicians to ensure on time successful execution of technical requirements. Additionally we provide extensive project management expertise, providing onsite support, safe working procedures, project plans, work instructions, risk assessments, budget control methods and regular reporting. When a client site is being moved or upgraded the obsolete assets are often of a mixed age and value. Many assets will have valuable data held on them. If faulty or damaged, some will be considered hazardous waste. The decommissioning, tracking and handling of this equipment is critical for both in terms of compliance but also to ensure there is no disruption to the installation of new equipment. Engineers are a valuable and expensive resource especially at time of equipment rollout. The support of a technical logistics team at such times can save money and improve efficiency. Unwanted equipment can be data-wiped on site, where high security is required. Equipment will require decommissioning and moving to a collection point with minimal disruption to the working environment.

Asset Remarketing

Once all items have been inspected, tested, data-wiped, NTPL will use their extensive reach and experience to place each lot in the most suitable sector within the marketplace to ensure maximization of the your return on the refurbished assets. NTPL offers transparent nationwide re- marketing and redeployment services through asset auctioning, bulk resale, direct-to-consumer resale and electronic take-back programs. Our unique remarketing model integrates physical and online marketplaces to connect a wide network of authorised, audited remarketing vendors and offer you direct purchase as well as revenue share models.

Secure Data Sanitation

NTPL also delivers guaranteed residual data destruction. On-Site Data Cleansing for sensitive data, i.e., onsite data erasure and shredding services, meaning that your company and customer data need not leave your premises. You can even witness data destruction first hand.
