SERVICES

NxtRonikx Technologies is recognized as the experienced IT asset refurbishment, recovery and remarketing organization operating in Bangalore, India

SCOPE OF SERVICES :- NTPL offers a complete and secure chain of custody to ensure the physical security of your assets as well as the data, from the time they leave your location, through the entire asset refurbishment process in our sites and onwards to the point of resale. Our certified processes, secure facilities and security protocols protect your intellectual property and align suitably to your corporate compliance requirements, whilst ensuring you the best return on your assets.

Our specialised point to point asset recovery solutions include Data Sanitation, Data Wipe, Refurbishment and remarketing of the assets.
